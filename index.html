About Me





Hi There!



I'm Swathi S.Nayak, a front-end web developer who strives to achieve her goal filled with challenges. My way of learning is to explore and acquire new skills in my life. I am currently pursuing my second year of Engineering in the Department of Information Science at Sahyadri College Of Engineering and Management, Mangaluru. I am very determined in the work I take up in my life and confident enough to accomplish them.

Since my childhood, I have always been fascinated by the different pieces of art I notice around me. I have participated in various drawing competitions in my school life. My journey of exploring the field of art has never stopped to date. Drawing has always been my hobby and a mode of relaxation.

I have participated in the All India National Level Art Contest by Art Chitrakala and Indian Art Contest in 2020.
